vorg

Plain Text Organizer

vorg is a plain text file format and a ViM plugin that can help you get stuff done.

It's Simple and Readable

Plain text is the most versatile method to store information. The vorg format makes organizing your tasks and projects easy on the eyes as well as on your fingers.

It's Ready for Collaboration

The vorg format is designed to be portable between different editors. I use vorg to collaborate with people who are not using ViM and they can read and work with the format as easy as I can.

Superpowered by ViM

The vorg ViM plugin makes working with vorg files fun and efficient. Vorg files have TAB-enabled folding, custom shortcuts to add sections and tasks, and other goodies.
